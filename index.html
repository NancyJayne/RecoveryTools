Orders

Order Process

  1. New: paid order is waiting to be claimed, packed, or reviewed.
  2. Packing / Packed: prepare the items and check recipient details.
  3. Shipped: add the tracking number first. The customer tracking email is sent here.
  4. Delivered: sends the review/returns email and starts the 14-day completion window.
  5. Returns, swaps, complaints: set the follow-up status and add notes. Open issues block auto-complete.
  6. Resolved: add resolution notes. A delivered order can then re-enter the completion window.
  7. Completed: closes the order. Completed orders are archived after 30 days.
  8. Archive: use Archive now or Unarchive order to correct the active order queue.
  9. Timeline: records admin changes, auto-complete, archive, and unarchive events.
Use Open returns/swaps/complaints as the daily exception queue. Use Show archived only when you need older completed orders.

    Why Join Our Affiliate Program?

    Our affiliate program is more than just commissions — it’s a way to amplify your impact while supporting chronic pain education. A portion of all sales supports Pain Australia, helping fund advocacy and research to improve lives. By joining, you're not only earning — you're giving back.

    Whether you're a therapist, educator, or health-conscious professional, our program helps you build a sustainable, passive revenue stream outside of your day-to-day clinical or professional work. Share what you already believe in — and be rewarded for it.

    We’re a growing community of aligned health professionals committed to evidence-based resources, education, and ethical sharing. Your voice, influence, and knowledge matter. Let's build something impactful — together.

    💰 Earn Great Commissions

    Unlike many platforms, we don’t charge you monthly fees or take heavy cuts. Our structure is designed to help you keep more of what you earn, with some of the highest affiliate rates in the health and education space. Whether you're selling products, courses, or workshops — you're empowered to grow your income without added costs.

    Our affiliate structure is designed to reward your contribution:

    • Courses: Earn a generous 80% on any courses you create and sell.
    • Workshops: Keep 94% of ticket sales when hosting at your own venue — we handle the ticketing and promotion.
    • Physical Products: Receive 15% commission for every product sold through your referral.
    Commissions are paid automatically via Stripe.

    About Recovery Tools

    Recovery Tools was created by clinicians who believe that recovery shouldn't be confusing, expensive, or out of reach. Our tools are handpicked, tested in clinic, and supported by clear guidance — because we want you to feel better, move more freely, and take charge of your own wellbeing.

    Our Mission

    To empower everyday people and therapists with the resources and education they need to manage pain, restore function, and build long-term resilience through evidence-based tools, programs, and hands-on care.

    Who We Help

    • Everyday people dealing with aches, injuries, or chronic pain
    • Tradespeople, athletes, parents, and desk workers
    • Therapists looking for better tools and client education

    Why It Works

    All our products are backed by clinical experience, science, and community feedback. Each one comes with recommended exercises, safe usage guidance, and the ability to track your recovery journey inside our platform.
